Personality Test Results

The letters in my personality type ISTP-P stands for Introverted (I), Observant (S), Thinking (T), Prospecting (P) and Turbulent (T). In my opinion I don't think the test is 100% valid however it is somewhat true. As you can see the test says I am 100% introverted which is not true. I am an introvert but I'll give myself a 89%-90% introverted. As you can see in the image I am 64% observant which I believe is true. Observant individuals are highly practical, pragmatic and down-to-earth. They tend to have strong habits and focus on what is happening or has already happened. For thinking I am a 69% which i also belive is true, because I mostly focus on objectivity and rationality and prioritizing logic over emotions.


For prospecting I got a 53%, i believe it should be at least 60%. Prospecting individuals are very good at improvising and spotting opportunities. They tend to be flexible, relaxed nonconformists who prefer keeping their options open. My turbulent is 58% I believe that this is true. Turbulent individuals are self-conscious and sensitive to stress. They are likely to experience a wide range of emotions and to be success-driven, perfectionistic and eager to improve.
